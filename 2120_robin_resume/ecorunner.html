Ecorunner XI: A most efficient hydrogen car

The Eco IX is a super-efficient hydrogen powered car. I worked on the project with a team of enthousiastic students from the TU Delft. I personally worked on structural analysis during the design phase of the car, and had a major contribution in the manufacturing of the carbon fibre frame of the car. The project contained many technical challenges, but because the car was built in the time of the lockdown for Corona, additional limitaions were put upon us. Regardless of the tough circumstances the team and I managed to break a world record, having built the most efficient hydrogen car at the time.
